Markets

EUR/USD

Euro/US Dollar

This pair is the most traded currency pair in the market, representing the worlds two largest economies. It relatively has the highest liquidity of any forex pair, leading to narrow spreads and low transaction costs.

USD/JPY

US Dollar/Japanese Yen

This pair is the most traded and liquid pair. Its high liquidity results in low spreads and smooth order execution. With its moderate Volatility, often influenced by both economic data and geopolitical developments.

GBP/USD

British Pound/US Dollar

Known as "Cable", this pair is one of the oldest. Its known for high liquidity (slightly less than EUR/USD & USD/JPY), can be mor volatile than other major pairs and its driven by economic decisions from both the UK and US plus developments in the UK.
